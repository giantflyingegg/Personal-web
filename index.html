Dr Kieran Sweetman MBBS MSc

Hello! My name is Kieran. After a decade in the medical field, working as a physician in the NHS, I decided to take a leap of faith and transition into the exciting world of technology and innovation. During my final years in medicine, I served as an OPM registrar in a busy east London hospital. Since 2017, I've immersed myself in crypto and Web3, becoming an active participant in the Ethereum community and ecosystem. My journey over the past two years has led me to work with one of the most influential and innovative culture DAOs, fwbdao, and also attending a variety of Web3 and culture-centric conferences and events. Weekly Web3 Workshop

The WW3WS is a weekly meetup dedicated to the the web3 space. Founded in 2022 by James Zaki, a developer with the Ethereum Foundation, our weekly meetups serve as a vehicle for talented individuals to share innovation, discussion, and exploration. While our roots lie in the Ethereum ecosystem, we also explore the broader crypto universe, tackling topics that range from the latest technological advancements to the economic, sociological, and philosophical underpinnings of this digital revolution.
